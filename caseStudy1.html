Problem Statement

In the growing online education market, finding qualified tutors for specific needs is challenging. Students and parents often struggle with locating specialists, scheduling, and booking sessions, leading to poor performance and increased stress. LearnMate bridges this gap by offering a platform that simplifies finding and booking the right tutor for personalized learning.


The Goal

To enable Parents and students to hire teachers as per their convinience and get their doubts and concepts srystal clear right at their doorstep.



User research

Conducted secondary research relating to students preparing for competetive exams like JEE and NEET via online medium.

Pain Points

Of students

  • Watching online lectures for hours but not able to grasp the concept effectively due to lack of connect with the teacher
  • Plagiarized solutions to doubts asked in online class which may or may not be correct
  • Teachers not willing to give individual attention in order to rush through the syllabus
  • No persoal feedback on tests

Of parents

  • Not being able to find suitable tutors for their children due to various reasons like lack of time, availability, and the lack of a user-friendly platform
  • Not being able to provide personalized feedback to their children regarding their learning progress
  • Not being able to book sessions with the right tutors
